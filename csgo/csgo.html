CS:GO Objective

CSGO is an economy based FPS shooter with an attacking team and defending team. The attacking teams objective is to plant the bomb on one of two bomb sites or eliminate all of the defenders before the time runs out. The defenders job is too defend the bombs and not let the attackers plant. If they eliminate all attackers before the timer runs out they win the round.
